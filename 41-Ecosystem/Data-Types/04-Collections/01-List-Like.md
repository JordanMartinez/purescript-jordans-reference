## List (Strict)

```haskell
-- Data.List.Types

data List a
  = Nil
  | Cons a (List a)

infixr 6 Cons as :

-- example
1 : 2 : Nil -- Cons 1 (Cons 2 Nil) -- [1, 2]
```
